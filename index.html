Privacy Policy for PackReadyGo

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

Welcome to PackReadyGo, a mobile application developed by Mavix G Studios.

PackReadyGo is designed to help users organize travel packing lists, create reusable packing kits, and prepare for trips efficiently.

This Privacy Policy explains how PackReadyGo handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

PackReadyGo is designed with privacy in mind.

We Do Not Collect Personal Data

  • We do not require user registration or login.
  • We do not collect personal information.
  • We do not collect email addresses.
  • We do not collect phone numbers.
  • We do not collect payment information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not track users across apps or websites.
  • We do not sell or share user data with third parties.

2. Local Data Storage

PackReadyGo may store app data locally on your device solely for app functionality, including:

  • Trips
  • Packing lists
  • Reusable kits
  • Checklist progress

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. Internet Connectivity

PackReadyGo can be used without creating an account. The app does not require cloud synchronization or remote storage for core functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

PackReadyGo currently does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or user tracking services.

If third-party services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Children's Privacy

PackReadyGo does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Because we do not collect user data, the app is generally safe for users of all ages.

6. Data Security

Since PackReadyGo does not collect or store user data on external servers, risks associated with remote data storage are minimized.

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

Continued use of the app after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
