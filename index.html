Privacy Policy for PackReadyGo

Privacy Policy for PackReadyGo Effective Date: May 19, 2026 Welcome to PackReadyGo, a mobile application developed by Mavix G Studio. PackReadyGo is designed to help users organize travel packing lists, create reusable packing kits, and prepare for trips efficiently. This Privacy Policy explains how PackReadyGo handles user information. 1. Information We Collect PackReadyGo is designed with privacy in mind. We Do Not Collect Personal Data We do not: • require user registration or login • collect personal information • collect email addresses • collect phone numbers • collect payment information • collect location data • track users across apps or websites • sell or share user data with third parties 2. Local Data Storage PackReadyGo may store app data locally on your device solely for app functionality, including: • trips • packing lists • reusable kits • checklist progress This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. 3. Internet Connectivity PackReadyGo can be used without creating an account. The app does not require cloud synchronization or remote storage for core functionality. 4. Third-Party Services PackReadyGo currently does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or user tracking services. If third-party services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. 5. Children’s Privacy PackReadyGo does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Because we do not collect user data, the app is generally safe for users of all ages. 6. Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices. 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
